Terrarium

Ideal For: Indoor decoration, gifting

Best For: Home gardeners

Advantages: Easy maintenance, eco-friendly

Price: ₹149

About: A terrarium is a small, glass container used to grow plants indoors. It creates a mini ecosystem, ideal for both decorative and functional purposes. There are two main types: open terrariums for dry plants like cacti and succulents, and closed terrariums for plants that thrive in humidity, such as ferns and mosses. To make a terrarium, start with a container, add drainage stones, then place activated charcoal, soil, and finally, the plants. You can decorate it with small stones or figurines. Terrariums are easy to maintain and enhance the natural beauty of indoor spaces.

DIY Kit

Includes: Seed, Manure, Pot

Price: ₹200

About: A DIY planting kit that includes manure, a pot, and seeds is a simple and convenient gardening package designed to help you grow your own plants at home. It typically comes with a small pot, organic manure for enriching the soil, and seeds of flowers, herbs, or vegetables. The kit also includes instructions on how to plant and care for your seeds, making it ideal for beginners. To use the kit, you fill the pot with a layer of manure and soil, plant the seeds, water them gently, and place the pot in a suitable location for sunlight. It’s a perfect way to start gardening sustainably while adding a personal touch of greenery to your space.

Manure

Price: ₹100 - 1kg

About: Manure is organic matter, typically from animals, used as a natural fertilizer to improve soil quality. It provides essential nutrients like nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium, which are vital for plant growth. Manure helps enhance soil structure, retains moisture, and promotes healthy root development. One of the most common types is **cow manure**, which is nutrient-rich and great for vegetables. Composted manure is decomposed for safer use and is a great option for gardens. It is an eco-friendly, organic alternative to chemical fertilizers, making it a popular choice for sustainable gardening.
