How Can Agriculture Affect the Environment?

  • Promoting Sustainable Practices: Encourages organic farming, composting, and crop rotation, reducing chemical usage and improving soil health.
  • Conserving Biodiversity: Preserves local species and creates wildlife habitats, promoting ecosystem balance.
  • Reducing Carbon Footprint: Grows carbon-absorbing plants, reduces transportation emissions by supporting local farming.
  • Water Conservation: Implements drip irrigation and rainwater harvesting to combat water scarcity in farming.
  • Educational Awareness: Engages students in learning sustainable farming and environmental care.
